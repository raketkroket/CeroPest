Bees are protected – and essential

Bees are vital to our ecosystem. That's why we relocate bee nests whenever possible instead of exterminating them.

Only when the nest poses a direct danger (e.g. inside the home or for allergy sufferers) do we choose another solution. We work with beekeepers to give the bees a new safe location.

Safe for you and for the bees.

Signs of a bee nest

Identification signs

  • • Many bees around one spot (gutter, chimney, cavity wall)
  • • Visible honeycomb in an opening
  • • Buzzing sound from wall or roof
  • • Honey drips or wax residue
  • • Bees flying in and out of a small hole

Risks and consequences

  • • Painful stings (dangerous for allergy sufferers)
  • • Nest in home can leak honey and cause damage
  • • Stress for children and pets
  • • Nest can continue growing for years
  • • DIY removal is dangerous

Our eco-friendly approach

1. Thorough inspection

We safely assess the size and location of the nest from a distance.

2. Relocation where possible

In cooperation with beekeepers, we relocate the nest to a safe new location.

3. Aftercare & prevention

We seal openings and provide advice to prevent new nests.
