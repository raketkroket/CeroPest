Annoying and dangerous nuisance

Wasps are beneficial in nature, but a nest near your home quickly creates dangerous situations – especially for children, allergic individuals or on patios.

CeroPest removes wasp nests safely and effectively using professional equipment and protective gear. Often on site the same day.

Do not attempt it yourself – one sting can already be risky.

Signs of a wasp nest

Identification signs

  • • Many wasps around the house, gutter or shed
  • • Gray paper-like nest (football size or smaller)
  • • Wasps flying in and out of a small hole
  • • Aggressive behavior when approached
  • • Nest often in cavity wall, attic or ground

Risks and consequences

  • • Painful stings with swelling and itching
  • • Allergic reactions (anaphylaxis possible)
  • • Multiple stings when disturbing the nest
  • • Stress and unsafe garden/terrace
  • • Nest grows rapidly in summer
