A persistent and discreet problem

Cockroaches are extremely resilient and reproduce rapidly. DIY control is nearly impossible without professional products.

CeroPest uses advanced gel and spray methods that target all life stages (including eggs). Result: a cockroach-free home or business with minimal disruption.

Early action prevents large infestations and reputational damage.

Signs you should not ignore

Identification signs

  • • Brown/black insects (1-5 cm) active at night
  • • Musty odor with large numbers
  • • Dark droppings (like coffee grounds)
  • • Egg cases in dark corners
  • • Damage to food packaging

Risks and consequences

  • • Spread of bacteria and pathogens
  • • Allergic reactions and asthma
  • • Contamination of food and surfaces
  • • Reputational damage for businesses
  • • Rapid spread throughout the building
