A common and persistent problem

Mice are smart and fast rodents that reproduce rapidly. DIY control is often temporary – professional help is needed for a permanent solution.

CeroPest uses modern traps, bait stations and preventive measures to completely eliminate mice. Often on site the same day in case of emergency.

Don't wait – a few mice quickly become an infestation.

Signs of a mice infestation

Identification signs

  • • Small black droppings (like grains of rice)
  • • Gnaw marks on food packaging
  • • Scratching sounds in walls or ceilings
  • • Grease marks along baseboards and walls
  • • Nests made of paper, fabric or insulation

Risks and consequences

  • • Contamination of food with diseases (e.g. Weil's disease)
  • • Damage to cables (fire hazard)
  • • Spread of diseases
  • • Structural damage to insulation
  • • Rapid reproduction (up to 60 offspring per year)
